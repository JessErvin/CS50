Broccoli Casserole

Ingredients Directions
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 8 ounces cheese wiz
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked regular long-grain white rice
  1. Stir the broccoli in the skillet and cook until it's tender-crisp, stirring occasionally. Stir in the soup, cheese sauce and rice. Cook and stir until the cheese is melted. Pour the broccoli mixture into a 2-quart shallow baking dish.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 30 minutes or until the mixture is hot and bubbling.
