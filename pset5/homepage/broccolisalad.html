Broccoli Salad

Ingredients Directions
  • 1/2 cup Miracle Whip Original Spread
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. Heinz Distilled White Vinegar
  • 12 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 5 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled
  1. Mix Miracle Whip, sugar and vinegar.
  2. Combine broccoli, onions, raisins and sunflower kernels in large bowl. Add Miracle Whip mixture; toss to coat.
  3. Refrigerate several hours. Sprinkle with bacon before serving.
