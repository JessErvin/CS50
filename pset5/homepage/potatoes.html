Slow Cooker Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients Directions
  • 5 pounds red potatoes
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup half-and-half
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  1. PLace potatoes in slow cooker, add 1/2 cup of water for steam. Cook on high 4 hours.
  2. When cooked through, mash in slow cooker with butter and cream cheese. Add half-and-half till desired consistency.
  3. Season and leave warming in slow cooker until ready to serve. (You might need to add more half-and-half to get correct consistency.
