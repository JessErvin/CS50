Roasted Red Peper & Bacon Goat Cheese Dip

Ingredients Directions
  • 8 ounces plain goat cheese, softened
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped roasted red pepper
  • 3 slices thick-cut bacon, fried and chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 3/4 cup pistachios, chopped and crushed
  • 1 box Wheat Thins crackers
  1. Combine all except pistachios and crackers in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into serving dishes and top with pistachios.
  3. Serve with crackers.
